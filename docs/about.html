About Rooted

An independent app created to help plant lovers build a deeper connection with their plants. Built with care in Sweden.

A calm home for your plants

Rooted is a personal plant journal for tracking the lifecycle of your plants — from the first seed to the final harvest, and everything in between.

No accounts required. No cloud sync. No clutter. Just your plants, your photos, and your timeline — stored safely on your device.

🌱 Track & Organise 📖 Plant Journal 🩺 Plant Health Check 🛡️ Local-first 💧 Watering Reminders
🌿

Everything you need, nothing you don't

Rooted is designed around how you actually care for plants.

🌿

Track & Organise

Keep all your plants and their life stages in one beautiful place. Edibles, houseplants, herbs, flowers — each with a profile that makes sense for how they grow.

📖

Plant Journal

Log watering, repotting, stage changes, and freeform notes. Your plant's story builds itself over time — photos included.

📸

Photo Timeline

Capture your plants at every stage. Set a cover photo, attach photos to events, and watch your plant's journey unfold visually.

💧

Watering Reminders

Set per-plant watering intervals and choose your preferred time of day. Rooted schedules reminders so nothing gets thirsty and forgotten.

☁️

Backup & Restore

Export a complete snapshot of all your plants, events, and photos as a single zip file. Restore it at any time — fully, with no data loss.

🛡️

Your Data, Yours

All data lives on your device. No accounts, no tracking, no ads. You're in complete control of your plant history.

AI that assists, never decides

When you choose to use AI features, Rooted sends photos and plant data to OpenAI to help you understand your plants better.

AI can analyse plant photos for stage and health, generate growing guides tailored to your plant's profile, and identify unknown plants from a photo or name.

Every AI result is shown for your review before anything is saved. You stay in control — always.

📸 Photo Analysis 📖 Growing Guides 🔍 Plant Identification ✅ Human-in-the-loop
🤖

Accuracy, control & safety

AI is a helpful companion — not a replacement for your own judgment.

⚠️

Accuracy & Limitations

AI suggestions are a helpful starting point, not a definitive diagnosis. Results depend on photo quality, lighting, and plant species. Always use your own judgement.

✅

Your Control

You choose when AI is used. Every result is shown for review before it's saved. You can disable AI entirely by simply not providing an API key.

📷

Data Usage

Photos sent for analysis are processed by OpenAI's API and are not stored by Rooted. Rooted itself collects no analytics or user data. AI features require your own OpenAI API key.

🛡️

Safety

Growing guides and diagnoses are AI-generated and may not be accurate. Before applying any advice, consider your plant's specific conditions and consult a specialist if in doubt.
