Help Test Rooted Before Launch

Rooted is a privacy-first plant care app currently in testing on Google Play. Help shape the first release by testing features, reporting bugs, and sharing feedback.

🌱 Become a Tester Learn How Testing Works

What is Rooted?

A calm, personal app for tracking the lifecycle of your plants — from seed to harvest and beyond.

🌿

Track & Organise

Keep all your plants in one place. Log stages, notes, and history over time.

📸

Photo Progress

Add photos over time to see how your plants grow. Attach images to events and timelines.

💧

Watering & Reminders

Track watering history and set reminders so you never forget a plant again.

🧠

Optional AI Features

Use your own OpenAI API key for AI-powered plant analysis, health checks, and growing guides.

🔒 Your data stays on your device. Rooted does not collect, sell, or track your plant data. Everything is stored locally.

Who Can Join?

Testing is open to anyone who wants to help improve Rooted before the public launch. You do not need to be a plant expert or a developer.

  • Have an Android phone
  • Have access to the Google Play Store
  • Use a Google account
  • Be willing to install a testing version of the app
  • Stay opted in during the testing period if possible
  • Open the app a few times and explore features
  • Share occasional feedback if something feels off
💚 You do not need to be technical to help test Rooted. If you enjoy plants and can tap around an app, you're a great tester.

Join the Test

Getting access is simple. Follow the steps below — it only takes a few minutes.

1

Send your Google Play email

Email us at hello@rootedlabs.app with the Google account email you use with Google Play.

2

Wait to be added

We will add you to the Rooted tester list in Google Play Console. This is usually done within a day or two.

3

Receive the testing link

Once you are added, you will receive a Google Play testing link by email.

4

Become a tester

Open the testing link and tap "Become a tester" on the Google Play page.

5

Install Rooted

After joining the test, install Rooted from Google Play like any other app.

6

Start testing

Create a plant, try the features, and send feedback if anything feels broken, confusing, or unclear.

🔒 Privacy First

Rooted is designed to keep your data on your device. The app does not collect your plant history, photos, or personal usage data.

Some optional AI-powered features may send user-selected content to OpenAI when explicitly initiated by you. You are always in control of when and what is sent.

When sending feedback, avoid including sensitive personal information. Feedback is voluntary and handled manually.

Known Limitations

Rooted is still under active development. Here is what to keep in mind during testing:

  • Some UI may change between versions
  • AI-generated advice may be inaccurate — always use your own judgement
  • Backup and restore should be tested carefully, as restore replaces all local data
  • Minor bugs and rough edges may exist
  • Features marked as optional may require your own OpenAI API key

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to pay?

No. Testing is completely free.

Do I need to keep the app installed?

Please stay opted in during the testing period if possible. Google Play requires a minimum number of active testers to keep the testing program open.

Can I leave the test later?

Yes. You can opt out of testing at any time through the Google Play Store.

Will my data be visible to the developer?

No. Rooted stores your plant data locally on your device. Nothing is sent to any server unless you manually send feedback.

What if the install link does not work?

Make sure you are signed into Google Play using the same Google account you provided when requesting tester access.
